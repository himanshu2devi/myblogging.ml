My favourite destination to travel



I love visiting Mahableshwar and Panchgani whenever I need a break from life.Since it is situated in the heart of Sahyadri hills in Satara district,which is very close to my hometown and its just a couple of minutes drive .The place got its name derived from a Lord Mahadev temple and threeSanskrit words,Maha(great),Bal(power) and Ishwar(God)

Mahableshwar has many exotic viewpoints for spectacular access to ultimate facinating landscapes.Looking down from Mahableshwar one can see a panoramic view of the sea and valley.Besides cool atmosphere,it has calming influence because of the natural surrounding.If u love walking ,u can go trekking.The place is dotted with many waterfalls.It is the best place for family retreat.Also,u can visit mapro garden.Mahbleshwar is famous fr strawberries .Mapro garden includes beverages of almost all flavours but it has a speciality of the most famous strawberry crush .It also has falero Strawberry candies,with now addition of other falvours like mango,guava etc.

There is also a well known market in mahableshwar where people can go shopping and buy beautiful clothes,footwear,etc.Boating in Venna lake along with family is the best experience.I always enjoy the view from Sydney point in Panchgani.

A must visit place!!!Also do no forget to carry your digital cameras!

- Aastika Chopra
